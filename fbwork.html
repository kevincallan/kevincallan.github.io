Overview of my work in Facebook

During my time in Facebook I have worked across two teams.

Technical Data Analyst, Cross Platform Analytics

  • Research and quantify known and unknown risks and vulnerabilities across all ad products on Facebook. Working on the order of billions of rows.
  • Drive research and insights across teams of technical data analyst delivering to partners in engineering, product, legal policy & ops.
  • Develop research and experimentation best practice.
  • Roadmap & Coordinate across the team’s stakeholder map.
  • Build data infrastructure to power analysis mostly through batch processed jobs.

    • Technical Associate, Commerce Operations

  • Building and improving operational efforts for all of commerce on Facebook; Including products like Marketplace, Instagram Shopping, Shops and What’s App Commerce.
  • Collaborating cross functionally and globally with stakeholders from engineering, product, legal and care teams to drive integrity improvements.
  • Designing, building and implementing processes to improve the analysis efficiency and efficacy of the team.
  • Operational metric design to support the launch and maintenance of products to track success and performance of the business including sampling methodology and calculation process.
  • Creating documentation, notes, dashboards & visualisations to tell the story behind quantitative research that is aimed at technical & non technical audiences.
  • Performing ETL by building data pipelines to service ops’ data requirements.
  • Designing experiments and A/B tests to determine the effect of changes and improvements to commerce integrity products.
  • A significant part of my role at Facebook involves teaching, mentoring and sharing knowledge across various teams in global ops in an effort to continue building an analytics culture in global operations at Facebook.
